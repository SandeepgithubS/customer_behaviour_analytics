# Customer Behavior Analytics Dashboard 📊 | Power BI

A comprehensive Power BI dashboard focused on turning raw transactional data into actionable business insights — with strong emphasis on revenue sustainability, customer retention, and churn prevention.
It integrates multiple analytical layers such as RFM segmentation, cohort analysis, and customer lifecycle tracking to provide a 360° view of customer behavior.
The solution enables businesses to identify high-value customers, at-risk segments, and growth opportunities with precision.
Advanced KPIs and dynamic visualizations help uncover hidden patterns in purchasing behavior, loyalty engagement, and profitability trends.
The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making across marketing, sales, and operations teams.
It highlights early warning signals for churn, allowing proactive retention strategies to be implemented.
By analyzing customer journeys, it helps optimize conversion funnels and repeat purchase behavior.
The inclusion of loyalty program analytics provides insights into its effectiveness in driving long-term value.
Cohort and retention analysis enable businesses to understand long-term customer value and acquisition quality.
Overall, the project demonstrates a strong blend of business acumen, analytical thinking, and technical expertise, making it suitable for real-world enterprise-level applications.

##  Business Objectives

This dashboard helps answer high-impact business questions:

- Is our growth **sustainable** or seasonal/speculative?
- Are **loyal customers** really driving disproportionate revenue?
- Which customer segments are **most profitable** vs **value-destroying**?
- Why are customers **churning** and **when** do they leave?
- Does the **loyalty program** actually pay for itself?
- Which **cohorts** bring long-term value vs one-time buyers?

- ## Dashboard Pages (Business-Focused Structure)

| Page # | Page Name                        | Core Business Question                              | Main Outcomes / Actions Enabled                              |
|-------|----------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1     | Executive Overview               | Is the business growing sustainably?                | Track overall health • Prioritize high-performing categories   |
| 2     | Customer Purchase Behavior       | Who are our most valuable customers?                | Optimize discounts • Improve cross-sell & recommendations      |
| 3     | Loyalty & Membership Analysis    | Is the loyalty program increasing revenue/retention?| Justify program investment • Redesign rewards & tiers          |
| 4     | Retention Analysis               | Why do customers stay? Where do they drop off?      | Strengthen onboarding • Increase lifecycle engagement          |
| 5     | Churn Analysis                   | Why & which customers are leaving?                  | Proactive churn prevention • Protect high-value relationships  |
| 6     | Cohort Analysis                  | Which acquisition periods/channels bring best LTV?  | Optimize marketing spend • Improve acquisition quality         |
| 7     | Customer Segmentation (RFM)      | Who are our champions, at-risk, and lost customers? | Hyper-targeted campaigns • Maximize CLV • Reduce risky churn   |

## Key Features & Analytics Capabilities

- **MoM / YoY** growth & trend comparisons
- **RFM Segmentation** (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
- **Cohort-based retention & LTV curves**
- **Rolling retention**, **day-30 / day-90 retention**
- **Churn risk flags** & behavioral early-warning signals
- **Discount impact** on margin & profitability
- **Loyalty points** issuance, redemption & burn rate
- **Customer lifecycle funnel** visualization
- **Interactive drill-downs**, cross-filtering & bookmarks
- **Dynamic date slicers** & what-if parameters (where applicable)
